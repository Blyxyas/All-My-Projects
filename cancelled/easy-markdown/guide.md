⚠️ You can change your prefix (-) by changing the variable `pREFIX` in `markdown.js`

`-t` ↓ <h1>↑ Title</h1>
    `-t2` ↓ <h2>↑ Subtitle </h2>
    `-t3` ↓ <h3>↑ XXL Text </h3>
    `-t4` ↓ <h4>↑ XL Text </h4>
    `-t5` ↓ <h5>↑ ~ Text Size </h5>
    `-t6` ↓ <h6>↑ Small Text </h6>
